Authenticity & Counterfeit Awareness

At Ayuhthat we take pride in sourcing, processing and bottling our castor oil right here in Jamaica. Our products are 100 % Jamaican and trusted for generations【962632955126257†L94-L98】. Unfortunately, fake Jamaican castor oil exists – up to 70 % of products sold abroad may not originate from Jamaica【713730680423052†L82-L110】. Educated consumers are our best defence against counterfeiting.

How to Spot Real Jamaican Black Castor Oil

  • Check the label & packaging: authentic oil clearly states that it’s made in Jamaica and lists the ingredients (pure castor oil)【713730680423052†L92-L99】.
  • Colour & consistency: real Jamaican black castor oil has a dark brown colour and thick, viscous texture from the ash created during roasting【713730680423052†L122-L127】.
  • Smell: expect a roasted, nutty aroma – not a chemical or floral perfume.
  • Price: if the price is suspiciously low, be wary – counterfeit products are often cheap because they’re diluted or mislabelled【713730680423052†L127-L130】.
  • Red flags: products with a light colour, thin consistency or unusual smell may be counterfeit【713730680423052†L131-L134】.

Why Counterfeits Are a Problem

Fake castor oil undermines the authenticity and reputation of Jamaican black castor oil as a natural remedy. Mislabelled or diluted products can disappoint consumers and reduce trust in all brands【713730680423052†L82-L110】. By choosing Ayuhthat and other reputable producers, you support local farmers, traditional processing and the communities that rely on this heritage crop.
